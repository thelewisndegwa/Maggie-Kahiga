About Maggie

A Trusted Partner in Insurance & Mediation

Maggie Kahiga is a results-driven and highly trusted Certified Professional Mediator and Insurance Solutions Consultant with expertise in risk management, financial protection, and dispute resolution.

She specializes in helping individuals, families, and businesses resolve conflicts, protect assets, and build sustainable financial security through tailored insurance and investment solutions.
